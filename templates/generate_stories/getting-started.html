Getting started with generating customized kids stories.

There are two ways of getting started.

  1. Personalized Story Creation:
    Fill out a short form with some key details. At a minimum, you'll need to provide the child's name and what the story will be about. For an even more tailored experience, you can add extra details like the child's appearance, specific elements to emphasize in the story, or even a custom title. This will make the story more accurate and engaging for your little one.
  2. Predefined Categories:
    If you're in a hurry or looking for quick inspiration, simply browse our predefined categories and click "Generate." It's that simple!
