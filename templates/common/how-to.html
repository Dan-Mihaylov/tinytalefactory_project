How It Works

Step 1

Register to our website - you can create a new account or use your Google account to sign up | log in.

Step 2

To get a free token, verify your email - go to your profile, settings, email, and then add an email address to verify it.

Step 3

Go to generate story to create your personalized story.

Step 4

Choose how you would like to create your story - fill out the form or pick from pre-defined categories.

Step 5

Once you click generate, your story will be created if you haven't violated any terms.

Step 6

Your story is ready! You can find it on your profile or view it directly from the link provided.

Step 7

Click "View Story" to read and see the graphics of your personalized story.

Step 8

You can save your story and see how it will be printed in a PDF format by clicking on the save icon.

Step 9

Customize your story by changing background colors, character colors, and font size on all pages.

Step 10

Click "Download" to save your story as a PDF file when you're done customizing.
